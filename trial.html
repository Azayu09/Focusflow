About Us

We’re Error 404, a team of curious and creative students who love building cool things with tech. Our project started with one question: What if your screen could actually tell when you’re paying attention?

That idea led us to build an eye-tracking device that monitors where you’re looking and figures out if you’re focused or distracted. Whether you’re studying, working, or just trying to beat procrastination, our tool is designed to help you stay in the zone.

This project is part passion, part challenge, and 100% us—learning, experimenting, and pushing boundaries one line of code at a time.
